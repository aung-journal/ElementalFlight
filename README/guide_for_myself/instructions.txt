Main Menu Options:

    Play: Start a new game adventure.
    Instructions: Learn how to play.
    Settings: Customize your game experience.
    Achievements: View your achievements.
    High Scores: Check the top player scores.
    Users: Manage player profiles (if applicable).

Instructions:

1. Play:

    When you choose "Play," the game will start.
    You'll control the elemental creature's movement by tapping or clicking.
    Collect orbs of your element while avoiding obstacles.
    Matching the elemental orb with your creature's element doesn't penalize you; otherwise, you'll lose health or end the game.
    Achieving specific goals earns you achievements.

2. In-Game Play:

    During gameplay, you'll see your score and collected orbs.
    New obstacles will appear as you progress.
    Collect elemental orbs and activate temporary power-ups.
    The game ends when you run out of health or manually quit.

3. End Game:

    After the game ends, you'll see your final score.
    Choose "Restart" to begin a new game or "Main Menu" to return to the main menu.
    If your score ranks among the top, enter your name (up to 16 characters) to save it on the leaderboard.

4. Instructions:

    In the "Instructions" section, you can learn how to play the game.

5. Settings:

    Customize game settings such as sound volume and graphics quality.
    Return to the main menu after making changes.

6. Achievements:

    View all achievements and check which ones you've earned.
    Achievements may grant bonuses during gameplay.
    Return to the main menu after viewing achievements.

7. High Scores:

    See the top ten player scores.
    Compete for the highest score and bragging rights.
    Return to the main menu after checking the high scores.

8. Users:

    Manage different player profiles (if available).
    Useful for keeping track of personal high scores and achievements.
    Return to the main menu after managing user profiles.

Enjoy your journey in the magical world of Elemental Flight!
